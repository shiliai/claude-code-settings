## üí° Tips for Best Results

1. **Word Order Matters**: Most important elements first
2. **Be Specific**: "Sony A7IV" beats "professional camera"
3. **Lighting is Key**: Always specify lighting style
4. **Color Control**: Use HEX codes for precise colors (#FF6B35)
5. **Optimal Length**: 30-80 words works best
6. **Technical Terms**: "85mm f/1.4", "golden hour", "anamorphic"
7. **Artist References**: "in the style of [artist]" for specific aesthetics
